ATTA

ATTA is an Applied Capstone Project developed at Ashesi University in collaboration with Delasi Dake. It is an AI-powered Intelligent Tutoring System designed to address educational challenges in developing African countries like Ghana.

The platform leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized learning experiences, adapting to each student's pace and knowledge level to deliver targeted educational content.

About

Built using PHP and integrated AI components, ATTA represents the intersection of education and technology in Africa. The system identifies knowledge gaps and provides tailored exercises and explanations to help students master concepts.

This project reflects my commitment to using technology as a force for positive change, particularly in education across the African continent where access to quality tutoring remains a significant challenge.

Description

ATTA was developed as an Applied Capstone Project to tackle the educational gap in developing African countries. The intelligent tutoring system uses AI algorithms to assess student understanding, identify weak areas, and deliver customized learning paths that adapt in real-time.

The project involved full-stack development using PHP, database design for student progress tracking, AI model integration for adaptive learning, and UI/UX design focused on accessibility for students with varying levels of digital literacy. The platform was designed to work in low-bandwidth environments common across the continent.
